Since I was in fourth grade, I have played the Trumpet. Throughout my childhood schooling, I was always interested in music, and I think that had a part to play in my role as first chair trumpet through most of my concert and marching band positions. I think I was defined to a great extent by the numerous solos that I've had. I remember standing on the field of my high school football field during the half time show.

I had covered my trumpet in black and orange paint to show my personal spirit. Sweat dripping from my nose, I held the trumpet mouthpiece to my lips and started one of the most powerful experiences I have had. The sound carried to the entire audience and the crowd cheered. As I returned back into the formation of the entire marching band, I realized that I had just demonstrated the synchronicity that I think every human calls community.
